Methodology & data notes
Data sources
DC MPD hate crime Excel (1,823 events). MCPD all-crime CSV with lat/long + MCPD bias incidents CSV — unique ID VLOOKUP for coordinates. Merged and deduplicated.
Geocoding
DC: block addresses to coordinate lookup. MCPD: 67 rows by street match, 96 by city centroid. 101 DC rows had erroneous US coordinates — nulled and excluded from map.
Cleaning
Anti- prefix stripped. Dual-bias events split into 2 rows (+200 events). Latino/Hispanic, Arab/ME, Muslim consolidated. ADW/Homicide moved to Offense column.
Clustering (DBSCAN)
ε=1km · 30-day window · min 3 or 5 events. Identifies patterns consistent with a single actor targeting one group in a small area over a short time.
Prediction model
Exponential inter-arrival (Poisson) on 2023+ data. Location = median coordinates from 2025+ group events. 70% CI at 15th–85th percentile of exponential distribution.
Risk coefficient
= (events/100k population) × (mean severity 1–10) × (current events X-factor). Normalized 0–100. Not a law enforcement risk assessment.
